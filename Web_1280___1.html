If network is a container, then design is water in it
likewise. As a media, the form of network is determined
by science and technology. The birth of 4G has driven
the network to an unprecedented peak of development
– Social Network Services, live webcast, AI technology
and other emerging industries are born along with the
improvement of science and technology. As 5G
technology is expected to enter service in the next few
years, which means not only the acceleration of network
speed, but the possibility of change of network from 2D
space to 3D space as well. With the constant change of
the “container”, the design will change its role inside
freely, but its significance can’t be ignored. If the
meaning of the existence of design is to disseminate
information, then I believe, with the help of science and
technology, artists will create more brilliant art forms.
Let’s enjoy ourselves by that time.
